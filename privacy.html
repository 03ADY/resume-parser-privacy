Resume Parser Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 11/12/2025

1. Information We Don't Collect

Resume Parser is a local desktop application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.

2. Google Drive Integration

When you use Google Drive integration, the application requests read-only access to your Google Drive to process PDF files you select. Access tokens are stored locally on your computer only.

3. Data Processing

PDF files are processed locally using Google's Gemini AI API. Resume content is temporarily sent to Google's servers for analysis and is not stored by us.

4. Data Storage

Extracted resume data is stored locally in a ChromaDB database on your computer. No data leaves your machine.
